Great Moments in History

Photograph by Obed Zilwa, cover photo for Odyssey Magazine (Oct. 1996-Jan. 1997 issue). I post this as a tribute to the photographer and his subjects and their ability to inspire us.

 

In August, 1996, His Holiness the Dalai Lama visited South Africa and met with President Nelson Mandela.

 

When the Dalai Lama came south to Durban, I was in the audience when he asked us, "What are the vows you live by?" I'm still pondering that.
